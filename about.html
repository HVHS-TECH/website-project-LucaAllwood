About us

At St John Youth Petone, we are committed to empowering young individuals, ages 6 to 18, by providing them with valuable life skills, leadership training, and opportunities for community service.

Our core activities include:

  • First Aid Training: We offer comprehensive first aid training, equipping our youth with essential skills to respond effectively in emergency situations.
  • Community Projects: Engaging in a variety of community service projects, our members learn the importance of giving back and making a positive impact.
  • Leadership Development: Through structured programs and team-building exercises, we nurture leadership qualities in our members. They have the opportunity to take on leadership roles, plan events, and mentor younger peers.
  • Personal Development: We focus on the holistic development of our members, offering activities that promote physical fitness, mental well-being, and social skills.
  • Social Activities and Camps: To create a well-rounded experience, we organize fun and engaging social activities, including camps and outings.
