At St John Youth Petone, we are proud to have a dedicated and passionate leadership team committed to empowering young people and fostering a strong sense of community.

Our leaders bring a wealth of experience, knowledge, and enthusiasm to the program, ensuring that every member has the opportunity to thrive and develop essential skills.

Our leadership team at St John Youth also works closely with NCOs. Non-Commissioned Officers (NCOs) at St John Youth Petone are dedicated cadets who play a vital role in guiding and mentoring our members.

With their experience and commitment, NCOs help organize activities, lead training sessions, and support the development of their peers. They exemplify leadership, responsibility, and teamwork, making them an integral part of our vibrant community.
