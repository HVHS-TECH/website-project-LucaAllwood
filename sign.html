Join St John Youth Petone

Are you ready to be part of an exciting and impactful community? Signing up for St John Youth Petone is a fantastic opportunity for young individuals aged 6 to 18 to develop valuable life skills, make new friends, and contribute to their community.

Fees

While we do offer free uniforms, St John Youth asks parents and/or caregivers to provide for the children in their care.

Our fees structure is:

  • $25 enrolment fee; fee for joining
  • $25 term fee; fee each term
