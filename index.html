St John Youth - Petone Division

St John Youth Petone is a vibrant and dynamic division of the St John Youth program, dedicated to empowering young people in the Lower Hutt community.

Our mission is to provide a supportive and engaging environment where youth can develop essential life skills, leadership qualities, and a sense of community service.

Through a variety of activities, including first aid training, community projects, and team-building exercises, we aim to inspire and nurture the next generation of leaders.

Join us at St John Youth Petone and be a part of a positive and impactful journey!
